### Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed this Digital Literacy Portfolio to help peers navigate the digital world—from creating professional online profiles to communicating safely and responsibly. This repository contains work organized into five tasks, each mapping to a specific module of the course.

---

### Module Summaries

#### Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
* **Objective:** Created a one-page visual resource to help peers understand what digital literacy is and why it matters.
* **Topics:** Digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and professional online presence.
* **Tool Used:** Canva.

#### Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
* **Objective:** Established a foundational professional presence across key platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.
* **Details:** Updated academic history and created a specialized GitHub Profile README.

#### Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
* **Coding Practice:** Completed a beginner-level challenge on HackerRank to build familiarity with coding platforms.
* **Collaboration:** Developed a 5-question "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" using Google Forms to engage the campus community.

#### Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette
* **Email Communication:** Drafted formal emails for academic extension requests and internship inquiries following professional protocols.
* **Social Media:** Compiled a "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible social media use as a college student.

#### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
* **Case Study:** Conducted a detailed analysis of UPI and Online Payment Fraud.
* **Prevention:** Created a safety guide including reporting channels like the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.
