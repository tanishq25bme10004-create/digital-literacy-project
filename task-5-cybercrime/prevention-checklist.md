# 🛡️ 'Stay Safe Online' Checklist

1. [ ] **PIN Secret:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money; it is only for sending. 🔗
2. [ ] **Verify Identity:** Always confirm the identity of the person sending you a QR code or payment link. 🔗
3. [ ] **MFA:** Enable Multi-Factor Authentication on all banking and email apps. 🔗
4. [ ] **App Source:** Only download banking and UPI apps from official stores (Play Store/App Store). 🔗
5. [ ] **Sim Swap Safety:** Never share your mobile number or SIM details with unknown callers claiming to be from "service providers". 🔗
6. [ ] **Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid conducting financial transactions on public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks. 🔗
7. [ ] **Biometric Security:** Use fingerprint or face-lock for your UPI app for an extra layer of protection. 🔗
8. [ ] **Reporting:** If defrauded, immediately report the incident to the **National Cyber Crime Portal** ([cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)) and call the **1930 Helpline**. 🔗

---
> [!TIP]
> Keep this checklist updated and review your security settings at least once a month!
