## Reflection on UPI Fraud Awareness

## Key Observation

While researching UPI fraud, I was most surprised to learn how easily scammers use **"Request Money" QR codes** to trick users into entering their PINs.

## Changed Understanding

- I previously believed cybercrime mainly involved **complex hacking techniques**  
- I now understand that it often relies on **psychological manipulation**  

## Personal Takeaway

- Avoid scanning **unknown QR codes**  
- Always **verify the transaction type** before entering a UPI PIN  

## Conclusion

This task helped me become more cautious and aware while using digital payment systems, reinforcing the importance of staying alert to prevent fraud.
