# Task Overview
I designed a one-page visual resource using Canva to help my peers understand the core pillars of digital literacy.

# Reflections

- I chose to focus on **Safe Internet Practices**, **Digital Tools**, and **Email Etiquette** because these are the most immediate needs for first-year B.Tech students.

- Using Canva allowed me to practice **visual hierarchy**, ensuring that critical safety warnings are the first thing a reader sees.

- One interesting challenge was condensing complex cybersecurity concepts into short, actionable bullet points without losing their meaning.
