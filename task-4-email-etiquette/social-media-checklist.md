Responsible Social Media Use for Students:
| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Do use strong, unique passwords for every account. | Don't share your location or private travel plans in real-time. |
| Do verify news or links before sharing them with others. | Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or heated arguments. |
| Do keep your professional profiles (like LinkedIn) updated. | Don't tag others in photos without their explicit permission. |
| Do review your privacy settings at least once a month. | Don't click on suspicious links or "free giveaway" ads. |
| Do think about your digital footprint before posting a comment. | Don't post offensive content that could affect future jobs. |
