## Reflection on Digital Communication

## Scenario

One situation where poor digital communication caused a real problem involved a student missing a major internship opportunity. The issue arose because they used an unprofessional subject line and informal slang in their initial email.

## Key Insight

- Professional etiquette is not just about politeness—it directly impacts **credibility**.
- Small mistakes in communication can lead to **missed opportunities**.

## What I Learned

- Structure emails with **clear and relevant subject lines**  
- Use **appropriate salutations**  
- Maintain a **professional tone** throughout  

## Conclusion

This task helped me understand the importance of writing emails that are clear, respectful, and professional, ensuring that my message is taken seriously in a professional environment.
